## Source reliability levels

Use these labels in notes:

- `official`: ministry/CNEE or official exam document.
- `exam-document`: actual national exam or official correction.
- `teacher-reference`: teacher-made resource.
- `third-party`: educational website, YouTube, paid resource, or aggregator.
- `unknown`: unclear source.
